Account Deletion for Graadr

We believe in giving you full control over your data. You can request the deletion of your Graadr account and all associated data at any time.

How to Request Account Deletion

You can request account deletion using one of the following methods:

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

  1. Open the Graadr app on your device.
  2. Navigate to your Profile or Settings screen.
  3. Tap on the "Delete Account" option.
  4. Follow the on-screen prompts to confirm the deletion. This is the fastest method.

Method 2: Email Request

If you are unable to access the app, you can send an email to our support team at support@graadr.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". Please include the username or email address associated with your account so we can identify it.

What Happens When Your Account is Deleted?

When you request the deletion of your account, the following data will be permanently and irreversibly removed from our systems:

  • Your user profile (username, email address, etc.)
  • All photos you have uploaded
  • All ratings you have received
  • All ratings you have given to others

Please note that some non-identifiable, anonymized data (such as aggregated app performance metrics) may be retained for analytical purposes. This data cannot be linked back to you in any way.

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 14 business days.
