Welcome to Graadr ("App," "Service," "we," "us," "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

When you use Graadr, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, password, and profile information

  • User-Generated Content: Ratings, comments, reviews, and other content you create
  • Communication Data: Messages you send through our support channels

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

We automatically collect certain information when you use our Service:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, mobile network information
  • Usage Data: App features used, time spent in the app, interaction patterns, crash reports
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, access times, pages viewed, and the page you visited before navigating to our Service

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication: If you sign up using Google, Apple, or other social media accounts
